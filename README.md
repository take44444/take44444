## 👋 Hi, there

I'm Takeshi Masumoto, a software developer. I currently work as a architect specializing in the telecommunications domain. With experience in the field since my university days, I've had the opportunity to work on various exciting projects and explore different technologies.

## 🚀 Expertise

### Architectre

I currently work as an architect for an operation support systems (OSS) product, and have knowledge of common architectural best practices such as Domain-Driven Design (DDD) and clean architecture, as well as knowledge of OSS architecture standardized by [TM Forum](https://www.tmforum.org/).

### Computer Architecture

I have knowledge of computer architecture, gained from my research specializing in security and my activities in a robot contest club at university. I published a book on creating a Game Boy emulator, and it has sold over 700 copies. I am grateful for the many positive comments I have received 🙏

I am a graduate of [Security Next Camp](https://www.ipa.go.jp/jinzai/security-camp/next.html) 2020.

### Algorithm

I enjoyed competitive programming while I was a graduate student. I have knowledge of various data structures and algorithms (especially graph algorithms) and practical experience in the contests. I publish web pages explaining various algorithms, and the page on [Splay tree](https://take44444.github.io/Algorithm-Book/range/bbst/splay-tree/main.html) is particularly popular.

I am a cyan coder in [Atcoder](https://atcoder.jp/).

### Computer Graphics

I develop and distribute AviUtl plugins that use noise algorithms such as Perlin noise, fractal noise, and curl noise. I also have experience in shader programming.
